About Me

I'm a second-year student at Fathalla International Applied Technology School (IATS), under the USAID-funded Workforce Egypt Project, majoring in Software Development Programming.

With a background as a professional photographer, I pivoted into programming to expand my creative expression and tackle real-world problems through technology. I'm fluent in Arabic (Egyptian dialect) and continuously developing my English skills.

I'm known for being highly social, empathetic, and passionate about using technology to empower people. I believe in creating solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

My journey combines technical expertise with creative vision, allowing me to approach problems from unique perspectives. Whether I'm coding an innovative solution or capturing moments through my lens, I'm committed to excellence and continuous growth.
